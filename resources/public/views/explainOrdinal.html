Part 2 — Relative importance of one treatment outcome over another

In the second part of the survey, you will be asked to consider a hypothetical treatment that performs relatively poorly with respect to each of the included outcomes.

You will also be provided with a list of improvements that you can use to upgrade (step-by-step) the performance of this treatment until you have constructed a treatment that performs relatively well with respect to all these outcomes.

The order in which you select these improvements determines how you value the different outcomes from most important to least important.
